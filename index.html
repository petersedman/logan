Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GLP-1 treatments

GLP-1 treatments are typically suitable for adults aged 18-85 with a BMI of 30 or above, or a BMI of 27 or above with weight-related health conditions such as type 2 diabetes or high blood pressure. Different BMI thresholds may apply for certain ethnic backgrounds.

Both medications work by mimicking hormones that regulate appetite. Wegovy contains semaglutide which mimics GLP-1 hormone. Mounjaro contains tirzepatide which mimics both GLP-1 and GIP hormones, potentially offering enhanced weight loss. They help you feel fuller for longer and reduce cravings.

Common side effects include nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and constipation. These are usually mild and tend to settle within the first few weeks of treatment. Starting at a lower dose and gradually increasing helps minimise side effects. Our pharmacists will guide you through managing any symptoms.

Both Wegovy and Mounjaro are administered as a once-weekly self-injection. The injection is given under the skin (subcutaneously) in the stomach, thigh, or upper arm. Our pharmacists will teach you the proper injection technique during your consultation.

Clinical trials show average weight loss of 15% with Wegovy and up to 21% with Mounjaro over 68-72 weeks. Individual results vary based on factors including diet, exercise, and adherence to treatment. Best results are achieved when combined with lifestyle changes.

During your consultation, our pharmacist will review your questionnaire responses, discuss your health history and goals, explain the treatment options in detail, answer any questions you have, and if appropriate, provide a prescription and guide you through the injection technique.

Yes, both Wegovy and Mounjaro are approved by the MHRA (UK medicines regulator) and FDA. They have been extensively studied in clinical trials. However, they may not be suitable for everyone, which is why we conduct a thorough health assessment before prescribing. Certain conditions may preclude treatment.

Prices start from £149/month for Wegovy and £199/month for Mounjaro. This includes your medication, pharmacist consultations, and ongoing support. Payment is taken monthly. We accept all major credit and debit cards. Full pricing details will be discussed during your consultation.
